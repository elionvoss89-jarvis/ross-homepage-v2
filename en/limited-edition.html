Limited Edition Philosophy

Art in limited edition

Ross Limited Edition offers design collections the way an art gallery offers signed lithographs — in limited numbered series with only 12 copies of each model. Every villa is an art book, every design a masterpiece, every timeless form an investment in the future and your family's happiness.

You can choose a collection exactly as it is presented — or have it customized to your preferences. Either way gives you a home that is both unique and part of an exclusive community of visions.
