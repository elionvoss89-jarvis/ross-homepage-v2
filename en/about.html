About Ross

Art and function
in harmony

Award-winning architecture firm since 1996. Innovative, Scandinavian creations that combine art, function, and quality in harmony with nature.

Philosophy

Architecture is more
than buildings — it is art

Each villa from Ross harmonizes with its surroundings and creates a protective frame where humans and nature coexist. We strive for aesthetic and technical solutions that prioritize your wellbeing.

Founder

Pål Ross

Architect SAR/MSA, founder and creative leader. Since 1996, Pål Ross has created over 350 villas. His signature: sculptural forms, white stone houses that blend seamlessly with nature. Appointed as a member of the Artists' Club.

Consultations take place in our studio at Hertigarnas Stall at Drottningholm Palace, Stockholm. We also meet with you digitally if that works better.

Qualities

A Ross Home

Every Ross home is a climate-smart and award-winning work of art. We create stone houses with cast exterior walls and superior insulation — buildings that defy time and the elements. With open floor plans and private balcony zones, we create flexible homes for families who value both togetherness and privacy.

Energy efficiency is not a trend for us — it's a commitment. We integrate the house completely with the landscape, use the latest technology for smart homes, and guarantee fresh, healthy construction. Your future lives in a home built to grow with you.
