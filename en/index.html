About Ross

Art and function
in harmony

Since 1996, architect Pål Ross and his team have offered unique and award-winning design. Our specialty is creating sculptural dream homes — exclusively tailored to your lifestyle and interests.

Consultations take place in our studio at Hertigarnas Stall at Drottningholm Palace in Stockholm. Our works are continuously recognized for their deep roots in Swedish architectural history.
