Re-Design

Transform your existing home into a work of art. We refine what already exists with the same thoughtful architectural excellence as new projects, creating spaces that are born anew from their foundations.

Re-Design Philosophy

Give your home new life

Creating something new is not always about starting from scratch. We reshape what already exists and give it an entirely new life. With the same care and dedication as a completely new project, we create spaces that bring renewed joy and hope for the future. Your home awakens from its slumber and becomes once again a source of inspiration, well-being, and pride.

Ross Re-Design is for you who love your home but long for change. We listen, understand, and transform with precision and passion.

The Process

Refine what exists

We begin with a deep understanding of your home and your needs. What do you love? What can be improved? Through conversations, measurements, and visualizations, we develop a vision that respects your home's soul while transforming it into the future.

Every detail is planned with precision. Materials, proportions, light, and function — everything is part of a greater art. The result is a home that feels completely new yet in its own way has been part of your family for years.
