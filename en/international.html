Every international project begins with a deep understanding of the place's essence. We study climate, culture, architectural history and the landscape itself. The result is design that respects local identity while expressing the timeless Scandinavian elegance that has made Ross known worldwide.

From calculations of sun angles at the Mediterranean to understanding Norwegian mountain landscapes — nothing is too small or too large for our dedication. We take the time to build right, not just quickly. That is why our villas are loved in six countries.
